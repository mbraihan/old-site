About

I am Mohammad Raihanul Bashar from Dhaka, Bangladesh. I've completed my BSc in Computer Science from Independent University, Bangladesh. I'm currently working as a Research Assistant at the Computer Vision & Cybernetics group under the supervision of Dr. M. Ashraful Amin. My research interests include, but are not limited to, Natural Language Processing, Reinforcement Learning, Cognitive Science, Deep Learning, Bayesian Machine Learning and Information Theory, Sequential Prediction, Dialogue Systems, Neuroscience and Artificial Intelligence.
